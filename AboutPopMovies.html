SOBRE A POP MOVIES!

          The website "POP MOVIES" is a TCM project from 2024 that brings together a wealth of information about the recent film industry. Here, you can explore everything about series, movies, cinema, awards, streaming platforms, and even the latest news about this industry.
          But that's not all—you can also dive into the international section to discover more about the history of international cinema and the origins of iconic aspects, such as why Hollywood is considered the city of cinema, among other internationally relevant features of the film industry.
